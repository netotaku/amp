Vanilla Ace

Man of many musical heads, Sam Young AKA Vanilla Ace passes through the studio to tell of his journey through the London club scene in Rap, Disco, House and everything in between. Talking influences, breaks, production, collaborations and comic collecting.

Episode 15

Dave Baby

This weeks Killa Kela Podcast is with Dave Baby: Artist , Model, Clothing Designer, Punk, sculpture, last standing member of House Of Beauty & Culture. Dave talks through his career spanning 4 decades, including his work as a saxophonist through the Punk era in New York and San Fran, member of the early 80's synth band The Transmitters, his work with Mark Lebon, his good friend and fashion legend Judy Blame, his erotic wood work design, work with Kate Moss and more.

Episode 14

Marger

This weeks podcast is with East London native, MC and artist Marger; reflecting on his early beginnings within his musical family & the early Grime scene - meeting Craig David, Dizzee Rascal, So Solid Crew and more, plus his colaborations on lead up to his latest EP

Episode 13

Tizer (I.D Crew)

This weeks Podcast is with one of UK graffs gems - Tizer of ID crew. Talking about his upbringing; his mother as a member of the Black Panthers and script writer, his introduction to Hip Hop, Punk, Skating and street culture, to his work in Graff ; writing across the globe, graffitiing Malcolm Mclaren's funeral box, the head offices at Google and much much more.. This ones a biggie!!

Episode 12

Jordan of Rizzle Kicks

Episode 11

Catherine Marks

Grammy Nominated and Music Producers Guild Award Winner Catherine Marks swang by at the top of the year to chat all things from production.. from techniques, to her work alongside Kanye West, The Foals, Wolf Alice and more.
